Tacos

Soft shell, mexican style tacos

The best tacos, with the original tortilla

Ingredients

  • Soft shell tortilla
  • Chorizo
  • Beef meat
  • Cilantro
  • Lemon
  • Serrano pepper
  • Green small tomatoe (tomatito verde)
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Water
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Toast the serrano peeper, the green tomatoe.
  2. Mix the toasted tomatoe and the serrano pepper with chopped onion, garlic and salt to make a green sauce.
  3. Prepare the beef and chorizo.
  4. Chop the cilantro and chop more onion.
  5. Heat the tortillas.
  6. Serve the meet into the tortilla, add the sauce and a little bit of lemon.

Pozole Verde

Tasty pozole verde

A great dish from Mexico, specially made for Independe day (of Mexico, of course)

Ingredients

  • Pozolero corn
  • Chicken
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Vegetable oil
  • Serrano pepper
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • 3 cups of water

Steps

  1. Skin the chicken and cut into pieces
  2. Boil the water
  3. Put the chicken on it until the meat is cooked
  4. Add the pozolero corn
  5. Add spices
  6. Enjoy

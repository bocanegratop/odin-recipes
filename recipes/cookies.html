Cookies with chocho chips

Everyone's favorite dessert, delicious choco chips

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Chocolate chips
  • Butter
  • Baking soda
  • Water
  • Sugar
  • Salt (just a little)

Steps

  1. Mix the flour with eggs, butter and add sugar and a little bit of salt.
  2. Add water in the baking soda.
  3. Stir the mix and add choco chips.
  4. Bake.
  5. Enjoy!
